Common questions

STRAIGHT
ANSWERS.

What if we need to pause?
Give 30 days notice and your spot is paused — no penalty, no questions. Life happens. Your locked-in rate is held when you restart within the 6-month window.
What program level does this cover?
The $250/month founding rate applies to the Development (Ages 8–11) and Performance (Ages 12–16) programs. Young Cup and Elite program families — reach out directly to discuss options.
When does the new facility open?
The Express Development Centre is targeting June 2026. Founding members get priority access from day one — your schedule transfers automatically, no re-enrollment.
Is this the same program as before?
Yes. Same 4-pillar curriculum. Same direct coaching from Grant. Same compete sessions capped at 6 athletes. The facility upgrades — the coaching doesn't change.
Why only 10 spots?
The Hockey Lab deliberately stays small. Compete sessions are capped at 6 athletes. Grant coaches every athlete personally. 10 founding spots is the maximum that maintains that standard.
What happens after 6 months?
You transition to the standard program rate ($399/month) — or you're free to walk away with no obligation. There's no auto-renewal trap. The 6 months is the commitment, nothing more.
