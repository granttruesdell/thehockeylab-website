The 6-Week Structure

What we cover, week by week

W1
Foundation and Self-Assessment
Understanding your mental game — where you are, what is holding you back, and what your personal performance patterns look like.
W2
Focus and Presence
Building the ability to stay present during competition. Pre-game routines, focus cues, and the tools to stay locked in when it counts.
W3
Reset and Recovery
The most important session. How to recover from mistakes mid-game so one bad play never becomes a bad period or a bad night.
W4
Confidence and Pressure
Building confidence that holds up under real pressure — not just when things are going well. Preparation-based confidence, not mood-based confidence.
W5
Composure in Big Moments
Tryouts, playoffs, big shifts. Specific tools for the moments that matter most, including breathing techniques, physical anchors, and mental rehearsal.
W6
The Mental Edge Blueprint
Everything comes together. Each athlete leaves with a personalized, written Mental Edge Blueprint they can use for the rest of their career.

Common Questions

What is the Mental Edge program? +
The Mental Edge is a 6-week performance mindset coaching program. It is not therapy. It is performance coaching — the same kind of mental skills work that elite athletes at every level use to compete consistently under pressure.
Who is this program for? +
Athletes ages 10 and up who are actively competing in hockey. Whether your athlete is chasing a spot on a higher team or just wants to be more consistent, the Mental Edge delivers tools that are immediately applicable to their game.
Is this 1:1 or group? +
Both formats are available. 1:1 sessions are fully private and personalized. Small group sessions (up to 4 athletes) are available when scheduling allows. Both formats cover the same curriculum and deliver the same tools.
How do I know if my athlete needs this? +
If your athlete consistently performs better in practice than in games, struggles to reset after mistakes, or their confidence seems fragile under pressure — those are the signs. Book a free 30-minute call with Grant and we will tell you honestly whether this is the right fit.
Is it included in Hockey Lab programs? +
Yes. The Mental Edge mindset component is integrated into the Performance and Elite programs as a monthly session. The full 6-week standalone program is also available at any time as an add-on or on its own.
What is the cost? +
The full 6-week standalone program is $199, which includes all 6 coaching sessions and the personalized Mental Edge Blueprint. There is no additional charge for ongoing support between sessions during the program.
